MISSION
My belief is that you can make your immediate reality however you wish it to be, but you must first let yourself imagine anything and everything that is extraordinary. Art is a bridge that connects the dream and the waking world. My goal is to help build it.

BIO
I started to explore my creative self during a very disorienting childhood. I kept drawing people, places, and symbols that I would see in the dream because I wanted to keep a piece of the dream with me during my waking hours. But as it is with most of us as we grow, the responsibilities and trials of daily life sometimes overwhelm us. So for a time, I was closed to the dreamworld and focused on survival and on building a stable life for many sleepless nights. I traveled thousands of miles to a different land. I severed ties, formed new friendships, and rebuilt broken bonds. Now, I am once again returning to my creative self and exploring the dream with art along with the ambition to close the gap between the dream and the waking world. I have been sleeping soundly and dreaming ever since. I currently reside in the Norwegian wilderness with my guardian cat and my best friend.
